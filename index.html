How your commission works

A quick guide — no finance background needed.

The big idea

You earn a commission on every recurring-revenue (RMR) agreement you sell — and again when it renews. Your commission is built from three things: how big the deal is, a percentage set by the contract length, and a multiplier based on how profitable the deal is.

Commission = Deal value × Term % × Margin multiplier. Every rate below is set by your administrator in the plan, so the numbers you see always reflect the current plan.

1 · The deal value

By default the tool uses Total Contract Value (TCV) — your monthly RMR × the full length of the contract. A $6,500/mo deal on a 36-month contract has a TCV of $234,000. (An administrator can switch this to Annual Contract Value — just RMR × 12 — in the Admin tab.)

2 · The term percentage

Contract termNew %Renewal %
12 months6%4%
24 months7%5%
36 months8%5.5%
48 months8.5%5.75%
60 months9%6%

3 · The margin multiplier

How profitable the deal is sets a multiplier on top of everything:

Gross marginMultiplierTier
60% and up×1.25Exceptional
55 – 59.99%×1.10Excellent
50 – 54.99%×1.00Target
45 – 49.99%×0.75Acceptable
Below 45%$0Not eligible
Below 45% margin the deal isn't eligible — it pays $0 until a manager approves an exception. Unlike the old plan, margin now affects your pay.

Put it together — an example

A $6,500/mo, 36-month deal at 62% margin:
$234,000 (TCV) × 8% (36-mo rate) × 1.25 (60%+ margin) = $23,400.
The same deal at 51% margin would be ×1.00 = $18,720.

Renewal commission

When an agreement reaches its renewal date you earn again at the renewal rate in the table above, using the deal's current value and margin. A 36-month renewal pays 5.5% of its value × the margin multiplier.

Autorenewal — on or off

ON — the agreement auto-renews and the renewal commission pays out automatically in the quarter it renews. Nothing for you to do.
OFF — the agreement shows in Pending Renewals and shifts color (green → amber → red) as its renewal date nears. You must manually renew it there to release the renewal commission.

Bonus Forecast & Pending Renewals

The Bonus Forecast tab estimates your upcoming renewal bonuses with reminder dates — it's informational only and never creates a payout. Pending Renewals is the action list of manual-renewal agreements you need to renew.
